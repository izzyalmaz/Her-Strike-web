Our Story

Founded in 2011 by Holly-Lace Ayton, Her Strike was created with the intention of being an online media platform to promote and support womenâ€™s football in Tasmania. The organisation quickly built a following and became highly regarded within the footballing community, demonstrating the demand for such a forum within the state. Between 2011 and 2017 Her Strike, under the guidance of Holly-Lace, successfully provided information, conferences, and clinics. Career opportunity led Holly-Lace out of the state at the end of 2017, leaving the medium at a standstill. After a 3-year hiatus Holly-Lace linked up with her creative partners, Adelyn Ayton and Izzy Ottavi, to rebuild and reform the Her Strike brand into something more purposeful and integrated.
